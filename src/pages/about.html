Our Journey

2015

The Beginning

Founded in a small garage by tech enthusiasts Alex Chen and Jamie Rivera, TechGo started as a custom PC building service for local gamers and creators.

2017

First Retail Space

Opened our first physical store in downtown San Francisco, expanding our product line to include laptops and peripherals.

2019

Online Expansion

Launched our e-commerce platform, allowing us to serve customers nationwide with our curated selection of premium computing devices.

2022

Award-Winning Service

Recognized as "Best Tech Retailer" by TechToday Magazine for our exceptional customer service and product quality.

Present

Continuing Innovation

Serving over 50,000 satisfied customers with three physical locations and a thriving online store, continually expanding our offerings.

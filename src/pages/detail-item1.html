The M2 Pro has a 12-core CPU and up to 19-core GPU, while the M2 Max has a 12-core CPU and up to 38-core GPU. The M2 Max also offers more memory bandwidth (400GB/s vs 200GB/s) and supports up to 96GB of unified memory compared to 32GB on M2 Pro. For most professional workflows like video editing, 3D rendering, and software development, the M2 Max provides significantly better performance.
